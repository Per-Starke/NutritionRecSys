Welcome to the Recipe Recommender! We hope you enjoy using it.
Go ahead and rate some recipes, so we can get to know your taste and then find recommendations for you.

A quick introduction:

To get started, you need to rate at least 10 recipes.
Before that, recommending recipes would not make much sense, because we don't have enough information to recommend recipes matching your taste.

  • When you click the "Recommendations & ratings" button, you get 3 recipe recommendations matching your taste from each of the two algorithms we use.
  • When you click the "Enter requirements" button, you can enter calories, macronutrients and mealtype you are looking for. Furthermore, you can enter a range, meaning how many percent the actual nutrient values are allowed to differ from the ones you entered.
  • When you click the "Matching recommendations" button, you get 3 recipe recommendations from each algorithm again, but this time not only matching your taste, but also the requirements you entered.
  • When you click the "Get a random recipe" button, you get a random recipe that you can try out, if you just want to explore recipes or rate more recipes.
